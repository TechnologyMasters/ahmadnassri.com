New name, same great content
After our successful 2017 and 2018 "Functions Conference" events, we're back this year as REFACTOR: Serverless!

REFACTOR is a series of community focused, single track, one day conferences. With the goal of fostering the community and helping all of us learn from each other as we embrace modern methods of building software, learn of emerging best practices, address the pain points for startups and enterprise teams. REFACTOR will have something for everyone.

REFACTOR 2020: Serverless features a mix of industry-expert speakers with curated topics, and local speakers from the Toronto community sharing their experience and lessons in building serverless applications.
